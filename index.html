Los Ninos In All Of Us

Our greatest impact is to offer a glimmer of hope for kids in need, longer than a moment.

Learn More

Mission

Los Ninos mission statement is to improve the lives of our Children by giving hope for those that are in need.

History

We are a dedicated, grassroots organic group started from personal life experiences – and evolved because everyone wanted something better for ourselves and other generations.

Who We Help

Since 2007, Los Ninos in All of Us has distributed more than 100,000 toys and gift cards through our The Toy Parties, over 25,000 backpacks filled with school supplies and gift cards through our Backpack in the Park, and raised $1,000,000’s of dollars donated to numerous kids, organizations and agencies serving Washington, Nevada, New York, California’s less fortunate.
