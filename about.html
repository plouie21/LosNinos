Mission

Our Mission is to improve the lives of our Children by giving hope for those that are in need.

Los Ninos In All of Us is also known as Los Ninos and was created in 2007. Los Ninos is a U.S. 501 (c) (3) exempt nonprofit, our employer identification number (EIN) is 81-4968142.
